Turkay

Turkey is a country of living culture, a country of ancient civilizations. It surprisingly combines the relaxing atmosphere of a beach holiday, lively nightlife, friendliness and hospitality of the local population, ancient history and developed resort infrastructure.

Himalayas

The Himalayas are considered to be the highest and most mysterious mountains of the planet Earth. The natural conditions of the Himalayan mountains cannot be called soft and stable. The weather in this area is prone to frequent changes.

Altai

The Republic of Altai is located in the very center of Asia at the junction of the Siberian taiga, the Kazakh steppes and the semi-deserts of Mongolia. This is a mountainous country with an extremely picturesque landscape in the center of Eurasia.
